Tap a letter to change it

How to play

Get from the top word to the bottom word. Each move, change exactly one letter to make a new word.

cold → cord tap a tile, type a letter, then ✓

You have 12 moves. Reach the bottom word before they run out. Fewer moves is a better score.

You can revisit words if you need to walk back.

A new ramble opens every day at midnight.
