How to grow a vine

Each day you get the same set of letter tiles as everyone else. What differs is how you arrange them.

  • 🌱Drag every tile from the rack onto the trellis to build one connected crossword. Every set of 2+ connected letters, across or down, must form a real word.
  • ✓When the rack is empty, tap Submit. If the board is valid, you get a sprout — a few new tiles to work in.
  • ✋Long-press a placed tile to grab its whole word, or keep holding to grab the whole connected vine and reposition everything at once. Drag a tile back to the rack to un-plant it.
  • 🌿Work each new sprout into your board and submit again. There are 5 sprouts a day; each one you grow is a point. Stuck? Recall and rebuild. Your board is saved, so come and go all day.
