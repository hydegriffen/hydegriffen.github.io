How to forage

Each day, players are presented with a new 5x5 board. Search for hidden words until you are satisfied and feel free to come back throughout the day.

  • ↔Connect adjacent tiles, including diagonals, to spell a word. Each tile can only be used once per word.
  • 4+Words must be at least 4 letters. Longer words are worth far more points.
  • ↗Drag across tiles and release to submit, or tap them one by one and use the "Submit" button.

Tiers

Your score is measured against every word hidden on today's board. Find more to climb from Scout all the way to Forage Master and share your results!
